Democratic Socialists of America

Whatcom DSA is an organizing committee of the Democratic Socialists of America, a political, activist organization that seeks to empower working people and marginalized groups and build a more equitable society for all of us.

Learn More

We strive to practice our principles in every aspect of our work; more information about those principles and how we act on them can be found in our bylaws.

Events

We believe that there is more to politics than just the ballot box. We are anti-capitalists who believe a better world is not only possible, but necessary. We work in solidarity with other community groups to advance our common goals. Come work with us for the good of all!

Member meetings are the second Thursday of every month.

Join Us

DSA is a dues-paying membership organization, not a political party.
